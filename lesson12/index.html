Site Description

This site plan is to explain the purpose and targeted audience for Scoots.com. We will share specific consumer scenarios, website structure and user interface organization. We will also coordinate relevant design and web page style.

Site Name

The name chosen for this site is Scoots.com . It will be a resource for scooter, ATV and Jeep rentals. It will also provide local information on where to ride vehicles. It will highlight business locations designated by the assignment as well as prices.

Mission Statement

The objective of our site is to provide tourist information and access to our rental business that is easy and understandable.
